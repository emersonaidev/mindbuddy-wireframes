Privacy Policy

MindBuddy Privacy Policy

Your privacy is our highest priority.

MindBuddy is built on strict privacy by design principles. We believe that mental health data is highly sensitive and users should always have meaningful control over what they share. From the start, we have designed our system to keep you in control and your data safe.

Key Principles

User control.

MindBuddy is designed to give you clear, meaningful control over your data. You decide what you share and when you share it.

Consent first.

We never collect any data without your explicit, recorded consent. You can manage or withdraw your consent at any time through our app.

Pseudonymised and encrypted.

All personal data is pseudonymised before storage and encrypted in transit and at rest. We do not collect messages, audio, images, or other sensitive personal content.

Transparency.

We clearly explain how your data is used, and you can access or export your records at any time. You can also choose to opt out permanently with no penalty.

International standards.

MindBuddy complies with global data protection laws, including the EU's General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), the US Health Insurance Portability and Accountability Act (HIPAA) where applicable, and other international frameworks.

Data Infrastructure

Server Location

All MindBuddy data is stored exclusively on EU-based servers to ensure compliance with European data protection standards and provide maximum privacy protection for our users.

Data Processing

Your data is pseudonymised using industry-standard techniques, meaning it cannot be attributed to you without additional information that we keep separately and securely. All data transmission occurs over encrypted channels using TLS 1.3 or higher.

Retention & Deletion

You maintain full control over your data. You can request deletion of your personal data at any time, and we will remove all identifiable information within 30 days. Research data that has been fully anonymised may be retained for scientific purposes in accordance with our research transparency commitments.

What We Collect

When you use MindBuddy, you choose to share:

  • Biometric Data: heart rate variability, sleep quality, respiratory rate, body temperature, and other health metrics from connected wearables.
  • Behavioural Data: screentime, app usage, movement patterns, social media frequency.
  • Contextual Data: city-level location data, transport mode, weather, ambient noise levels.
  • Self-Reported Data: mood ratings, panic events, significant life events.
  • Demographic Data: age range, gender, occupational category (optional).

How We Use Data

  • To provide personalised insights into your stress and mental health patterns.
  • To reward you fairly through our native $MNDY token system.
  • To support research partners in developing better anxiety and stress treatments.
  • To inform public health policy and awareness efforts.
  • To conduct our own internal research and development focused on improving anxiety and stress treatment.

Your data is never sold or shared without your consent. Researchers and partners access only pseudonymised, aggregated data through secure, permissioned systems.

Technical Safeguards

Encryption

All data is encrypted both in transit (TLS 1.3+) and at rest (AES-256). Database access is restricted to essential personnel and requires multi-factor authentication.

Access Controls

We implement role-based access controls, regular access reviews, and the principle of least privilege. All data access is logged and monitored.

Data Minimisation

We only collect and process data that is necessary for our stated purposes. Raw personal identifiers are separated from research data and stored in different systems.

Your Rights

  • Access your data at any time through your account dashboard.
  • Update or correct your records instantly.
  • Withdraw consent and request deletion of your personal data.
  • Port your data to another service in machine-readable format.
  • Lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority.

We believe privacy is not an add-on. It is the foundation of ethical mental health technology.
