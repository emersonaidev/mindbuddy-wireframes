Permissions

Health Access Required

MindBuddy needs permission to read your health data to track wellness and reward you with $MNDY tokens.

What we need access to:

Heart Rate & HRV
Monitor stress levels and cardiovascular health throughout the day
Sleep Analysis
Track sleep quality and duration to understand recovery patterns
Activity Data
Count steps, calories, and exercise minutes for daily goals

Your health data is encrypted and never shared. You can revoke access anytime.

How to enable:

1
Tap "Open Settings" below
2
Find MindBuddy in your apps list
3
Tap "Health" and enable all categories
4
Return to MindBuddy to start earning

You'll be redirected to your device settings
