FAQs

1 What is MindBuddy?
MindBuddy is an app that turns your smartphone or wearable into a secure, anonymised node for collecting real world stress data. By sharing your signals, you help build the world's largest stress intelligence platform, and you get rewarded with $MNDY tokens.
2 Why does research need real-world data?
Most anxiety studies happen in labs or clinics, with small and limited population samples. So, they often miss how stress truly unfolds in daily life. MindBuddy captures real-world signals over hours, days, and weeks, across all kinds of people and locations. This gives researchers, policymakers, and health systems the detailed data they need to create effective, personalised solutions.
3 What are $MNDY tokens and how do I earn them?
$MNDY is MindBuddy's native token that rewards you for sharing anonymised data. You earn $MNDY by contributing biometric, behavioural, and contextual signals consistently. Tokens can be used to unlock premium features, access exclusive experiences, participate in community governance, or exchange them on supported markets. It is a way to ensure the value of your data flows back to you.
4 What kind of data does MindBuddy collect?
MindBuddy gathers data across four dimensions: biometric signals such as heart rate variability and sleep, behavioural patterns such as screentime and movement, contextual information such as location and noise levels, and self-reported mood or events.
5 How does my data help?
Your pseudonymised data helps researchers study anxiety in real world settings, enabling the development of more effective treatments. It also supports public health planning, guides insurers toward better care models, and helps city planners design healthier environments.
6 Can I opt-out at anytime?
Yes. Autonomy is at the core of MindBuddy. You stay in control, with the ability to choose what you share and opt out at any time. Plus, all data is anonymised and encrypted with smart contracts recording your consent transparently.
7 Do I need a wearable device?
While MindBuddy works with many wearables such as Apple Watch, Fitbit, and Samsung, no wearable device is needed. While wearables will provide richer data, you can also track simple stress signals with your smartphone alone.
8 What makes MindBuddy different from other mental health apps?
MindBuddy is not just another wellness tracker. It is a decentralised, ethical, and user-owned data network. It rewards you for your participation, protects your privacy, and helps create the evidence base needed to transform mental health research and care.
