Security & Compliance

Security is fundamental to everything we do. Your trust is earned through robust technical safeguards and industry-leading compliance standards.

Current Security Measures

End-to-End Encryption

All data is encrypted in transit using TLS 1.3+ and at rest using AES-256 encryption. Your data is protected from the moment it leaves your device until it's safely stored on our EU-based servers.

Secure Infrastructure

Our infrastructure is hosted exclusively on EU-based servers with enterprise-grade security controls, regular security updates, and continuous monitoring for threats and anomalies.

Access Controls

Strict role-based access controls ensure only authorised personnel can access systems. All access is logged, monitored, and requires multi-factor authentication.

Data Pseudonymisation

Personal identifiers are separated from research data using industry-standard pseudonymisation techniques, ensuring your privacy is protected even within our own systems.

Compliance Standards

GDPR Compliant

Compliant

Fully compliant with the EU General Data Protection Regulation, ensuring your rights to data access, portability, correction, and deletion are protected.

Privacy by Design

Compliant

Privacy considerations are built into every aspect of our system design, from data collection to storage and processing.

Regular Security Audits

Active

Our systems undergo regular internal security assessments and we engage third-party security experts for independent penetration testing.

Incident Response

In the unlikely event of a security incident, we have established procedures to:

  1. Detect and contain incidents within 1 hour
  2. Assess the scope and impact immediately
  3. Notify affected users within 72 hours
  4. Report to relevant authorities as required by law
  5. Implement remediation measures and prevent recurrence
